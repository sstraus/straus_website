AquaUp

iPhone & iPad app · Apple Health · Hydration Reminders

AquaUp reads your sleep schedule from Apple Health to detect your wake-up time, then spaces gentle hydration reminders throughout your day at customizable intervals. A quieter way to stay hydrated, timed to the natural rhythm of your morning.

Core features

  • Sleep-based wake-up detection from HealthKit
  • Configurable reminder intervals (2h, 2.5h, or 3h)
  • Quick-log presets and custom drink amounts
  • Water intake synced to Apple Health
  • Interactive notifications with one-tap logging
  • On-device storage only, no account required
