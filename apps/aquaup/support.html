Support

Support for AquaUp is handled through App Store reviews and comments. Leave a review describing your issue or feedback and I'll respond there.

For anything outside App Store support, reach me through the contact channels on straus.it/#contact.

Troubleshooting

  • Make sure Apple Health access for sleep data and water intake is enabled in Settings > Health > Data Access.
  • If no sleep data is available, AquaUp uses your configured fallback wake-up time to schedule reminders.
  • Notifications must be enabled for the app in iOS Settings for reminders to appear.
  • Reminder timing depends on your detected wake-up time. If reminders seem off, check that your sleep tracking device is recording to Apple Health.
