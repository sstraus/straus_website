Live Steps

iPhone app · Apple Health · Widgets · Live Activities

Live Steps is a lightweight iPhone app that reads your daily step count from Apple Health and shows it in the app, on the Home Screen widget, and in Live Activities.

Core features

  • Daily step count from HealthKit
  • Widget with goal progress
  • Live Activity and Dynamic Island support
  • On-device storage only, no account required
