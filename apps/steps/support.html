Support

Support for Live Steps is handled through App Store reviews and comments. Leave a review describing your issue or feedback and I'll respond there.

For anything outside App Store support, reach me through the contact channels on straus.it/#contact.

Troubleshooting

  • Make sure Apple Health access for step count is enabled for the app in Settings > Health > Data Access.
  • Widgets may refresh with delay because WidgetKit decides the final refresh timing.
  • Live Activities and Dynamic Island require a compatible iPhone and must be enabled in iOS settings.
  • Health data is best tested on a real device, not in the simulator.
