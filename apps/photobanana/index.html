PhotoBanana

Mac · iPad · iPhone — Smart photo dedup & orientation fix

5 shots of the same moment. Your phone keeps them all. PhotoBanana keeps the best one.

PhotoBanana uses on-device AI to scan your Apple Photos library, group visually similar photos, and automatically pick the best keeper in each group. No cloud uploads, no account, no subscriptions.

Core features

  • Visual similarity detection — Apple Vision AI finds resizes, crops and retakes that file-level checks miss.
  • Smart auto-selection — face detection and quality scoring pick the shot where everyone is smiling and sharp.
  • Orientation fix — detects sideways photos from old cameras and corrects EXIF rotation.
  • Non-destructive — deleted photos go to Recently Deleted, recoverable for 30 days.

Privacy

  • All processing happens on-device using the Apple Neural Engine.
  • No cloud upload. No account. No tracking. No ads.
